*Before The Dawn* is an epic adventure about James, a shy teenage misfit who one day is pulled from his world — where he's ignored, bullied and forgotten — into another where he's needed, celebrated and respected.

Set in **Terrain**, a fantasy world similar to Earth but populated with mythical creatures and human-like Terrainians, *Before The Dawn* is part fantasy-adventure and part coming-of-age story. It follows James as he attains magical powers and combats the menace of the Overseers. Along the way he meets new friends and grows up to be a Master in his own right.

The first novel in a trilogy, *Before The Dawn* is original young adult fiction for anyone who loves fantasy or science fiction.
